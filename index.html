About

My name is Alec. I am a recent graduate with a Upper Second-Class Honours in Computer Games Programming from the University of Gloucestershire. I have lots of enthusiasm towards the gaming industry and am passionate about the code I create. While at University I spent 3 years developing my programming knowledge in multiple languages, including C#, C++ and Python. Above are a range of games and application I have produced. I aspire to become a C# programmer who works within Game Engines and also externally to write the scripts.
